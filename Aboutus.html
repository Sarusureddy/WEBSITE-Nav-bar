    Property description

    With a luxury spa and elegant rooms, Diya Resort features 3 award-winning restaurants. Situated in Brodick, on the beautiful Isle of Arran, the hotel also has a beauty salon and gym. Stylish rooms at Diya each have a flat-screen TV, free Wi-Fi and a modern bathroom with Arran Aromatics toiletries.. Rooms also feature tea/coffee making facilities. The leisure facilities include 2 indoor swimming pools, sauna and steam room facilities as well as a turbo spa. The Playbarn includes a large soft play area, a big-screen TV, a teen area with drinks and snacks available. The resort has up to 3 restaurants open, offering a wide range of dishes to suit all tastes. Set in impressive grounds, the Resort is a 20-minute walk from the Brodick Ferry Terminal.
